Summer 67-Day Photography Project 2016

From July 1 to September 5, 2016, I uploaded one photo daily to my Facebook timeline. Most of the photos are taken within this period spanning 67 days. The photographs capture moments of daily life, notably during my travels to San Francisco and Argentina this summer. Various types of landscapes, architecture, and people are featured.
